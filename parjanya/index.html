How it works

Just talk.
AI does the rest.

No typing, no reading, no technical knowledge. Press the mic, speak in Kannada or Hindi, get a spoken answer.

01
Press mic — speak in your language
Tap the microphone button. Speak your question in Kannada, Hindi, Telugu, or English. "ಈ ಸೀಸನ್‌ನಲ್ಲಿ ಯಾವ ಬೆಳೆ ಹಾಕಲಿ?" — the AI hears and understands.
