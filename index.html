Why Parjanya
"Parjanyāt annasambhavaḥ"
— Bhagavad Gita 3.14 · From rain comes food.

Indian farming is not failing because of lack of land, labour, or effort. It is failing because critical decisions are made without data.

When to irrigate. When to spray. When to harvest. When to sell. These four decisions, made correctly, can transform a farmer's income by 20–35% — without changing a single seed.
